# Vietnam — The Nine · 18–27 September 2026

**9 travellers · 9 nights · boutique / upscale · south-to-north**

Route: **Saigon → Hoi An → Da Nang → Hanoi → Halong Bay**

Flights are open-jaw — you land in **Ho Chi Minh City** and fly home from **Hanoi** — so the trip runs south to north. Two short internal flights (Saigon→Da Nang, Da Nang→Hanoi) tie it together. September is shoulder season — warm, lush, occasional afternoon showers; pack light layers and a compact rain shell.

## Day by day

### Day 1 · Fri 18 Sep — Arrive Saigon
Land at Tan Son Nhat at 16:15, private transfers to the hotel. Shake off the flight with an easy evening — rooftop drinks over the city and a relaxed welcome dinner. No alarms.

### Day 2 · Sat 19 Sep — Saigon
Morning at the **Cu Chi Tunnels**. Afternoon in town: War Remnants Museum, Notre-Dame, the Central Post Office, Ben Thanh Market. Group dinner + Bui Vien nightlife if the energy holds.

### Day 3 · Sun 20 Sep — Mekong Delta
Day trip to the **Mekong Delta** (Cai Be / Ben Tre) — sampans through coconut canals, river markets, tropical fruit. Back to Saigon for the evening. (Swap for a spa-and-shopping day if the group prefers.)

### Day 4 · Mon 21 Sep — Saigon → Hoi An
Morning at leisure, then fly **Saigon → Da Nang**, private transfer to **Hoi An**. Check in, riverfront dinner under the lanterns.

### Day 5 · Tue 22 Sep — Hoi An Ancient Town
The lantern-lit old town, **tailors** (order day 1 for a fitting before you fly north), Japanese Bridge, An Bang beach afternoon. After dark: a **lantern boat ride** and floating candles on the Thu Bon.

### Day 6 · Tue 23 Sep — Da Nang day
Choose your adventure: **Ba Na Hills & the Golden Bridge** + Marble Mountains, *or* a hands-on **Vietnamese cooking class** and countryside bike tour. Spa + pool to close.

### Day 7 · Thu 24 Sep — Hoi An → Hanoi
Final tailor fittings, then fly **Da Nang → Hanoi**. Settle into the Metropole, then a slow loop of the **Old Quarter**, egg coffee at Giảng, and a long Hanoi dinner.

### Day 8 · Fri 25 Sep — Halong / Lan Ha Bay
Private coach to the coast (~2.5h), board the **luxury overnight cruise**. Kayaking through karsts, a hidden cave, swimming, sunset on the sundeck, fresh seafood dinner, night squid-fishing.

### Day 9 · Sat 26 Sep — Bay → Hanoi
Sunrise tai-chi, brunch cruising back, disembark midday. Drive to Hanoi for the afternoon — Hoan Kiem Lake, the Temple of Literature, a final street-food crawl, then a water-puppet show.

### Day 10 · Sun 27 Sep — Depart
A leisurely Hanoi morning and last-minute shopping, then a private transfer to Noi Bai for the **16:00 flight home**. Tạm biệt, Vietnam.

## Practical notes

- **Visa:** Most nationalities need a Vietnam e-visa — apply online ~2–4 weeks ahead, all 9 together.
- **Internal flights:** Vietnam Airlines / Bamboo Airways. Book the **SGN→DAD** (Sep 21) and **DAD→HAN** (Sep 24) legs early as a group PNR.
- **Money:** VND cash for markets & street food; cards fine at hotels/restaurants. Carry small notes.
- **Weather (Sep):** ~28–33°C, humid, short afternoon rain likely in the centre. Light clothing + rain shell + good sandals.
- **Getting around in cities:** Grab (app) for taxis; pre-arrange private vans for the group on transfer days.
- **Indicative ground budget:** roughly **US$2,500–3,800 per person** (hotels twin-share, luxury cruise, internal flights, private transfers, guides, most meals) — excludes the international flights above. Refine once rooms are confirmed.

> Want a southern beach add-on? Two nights in **Phu Quoc** or **Con Dao** slot neatly at the start after Saigon if anyone extends.
