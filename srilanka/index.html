The intel

01 Visa

Sri Lanka ETA online before you fly — quick and cheap. Sort all seven in one sitting so nobody's stuck at immigration.

02 Weather

Late July–early August sits in the south-west monsoon on the south coast: warm 27–30°C, sunny spells with the odd downpour, and livelier surf (great for learning). Ella runs cool and misty — pack one layer.

03 Wheels

A private van + driver for the whole trip is the move — cheap, flexible, and no one has to stay sober to drive. Tuk-tuks for short hops; PickMe app in the cities.

04 Money

LKR cash for tuks, bars and street food; cards work at hotels and bigger spots. Pull cash on arrival and carry small notes.

05 Whales

Mirissa whale season is Nov–Apr, so your dates are off-season — don't bank on it. Surf, beach clubs and Coconut Tree Hill more than pick up the slack.

06 The Train

The Ella hill-country train books out — grab reserved seats early. Or just pile into a regular carriage and hang out the door like everyone else.
